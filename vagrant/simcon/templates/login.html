Welcome to the Simulated Conversation Project

This website allows researchers to create simulated conversations with videos and text responses. Researchers can then generate links to these conversations and email them to subjects for testing. After subjects step through a simulated conversation, which includes recording their audio responses, the researcher can look through the responses and listen to audio files for research purposes. This project was sponsored by the Department of Applied Linguistics of Portland State University
